Sleep Myths Debunked

Unlocking the secrets to peaceful nights.

The "Tired" Myth

"Keeping a baby awake longer will make them sleep better at night."

The Fact

Sleep begets sleep! An overtired baby produces cortisol, making it much harder to settle and stay asleep.

The "Silent" Myth

"The nursery must be completely silent for the baby to sleep."

The Fact

Womb-like white noise masks household sounds and provides a comforting acoustic environment for deep sleep.

The "Cereal" Myth

"Adding cereal to a bottle helps newborns sleep longer."

The Fact

There is no evidence for this, and it can be a choking hazard. Sleep is developmental, not purely about a full stomach.
